ASKED QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about Indian Visa Services? Check out our FAQs for quick and clear answers to all your queries!

What types of Indian Visas does Bharat Suvidha assist with?

Bharat Suvidha provides assistance for various types of Indian Visas, including Tourist Visas, Business Visas, Employment Visas, Student Visas, and Medical Visas. We also specialize in E-Visa applications for eligible nationalities.

Can I apply for an Indian Visa or E-Visa entirely online through Bharat Suvidha?

Yes, our service is designed to make the visa application process largely online. We guide you through the digital submission of documents and forms, minimizing the need for physical visits to any embassy or consulate. For E-Visas, the entire process is digital.

What documents are generally required for an Indian Visa application?

Commonly required documents include your passport, a recent photograph, proof of address, flight itinerary, and hotel bookings. Specific documents may vary based on the visa type (e.g., invitation letter for business visa, admission letter for student visa). Our experts will provide a tailored checklist.

How long does it take to get an Indian Visa or E-Visa processed?

Processing times vary significantly based on the visa type, nationality, and the workload at the Indian High Commission/Consulate. E-Visas are generally processed faster (within a few days), while regular visas can take several weeks. We will keep you updated on your application status.

Is Bharat Suvidha affiliated with the Indian Government or VFS Global?

No, Bharat Suvidha is an independent organization. We are not connected with VFS Global, the Consulate General or High Commission of India/Canberra office, or the Ministry of External Affairs India. We provide expert assistance to simplify your documentation process, saving you time and effort.
