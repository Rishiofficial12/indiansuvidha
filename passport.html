ASKED QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about Indian Passport services? Check out our FAQs for quick and clear answers to all your queries!

What types of Indian Passport services does Bharat Suvidha offer?

Bharat Suvidha assists with various Indian Passport services, including new passport applications, passport renewals, and updates for changes in address or occupation. We aim to make the entire process convenient and error-free for you.

Can I apply for a new Indian passport or renew my existing one entirely online?

Yes, with Bharat Suvidha, you can complete most of your Indian Passport application process online. Our experts guide you through the digital submission of documents and forms, minimizing the need for physical visits to any office or embassy.

What documents are typically required for an Indian Passport application or renewal?

Commonly required documents include your existing passport (for renewal), proof of address, proof of date of birth, and photographs. The exact list can vary based on your specific situation (e.g., minor passport, name change), and our team will provide a tailored checklist.

How long does it take to get a new or renewed Indian Passport?

The processing time for Indian Passports can vary depending on the type of service and the workload at the Indian High Commission/Consulate. While we strive for efficiency, it typically takes a few weeks to a couple of months. We will keep you updated on your application status.

Will my new or renewed Indian Passport be delivered to my home?

Yes, once your new or renewed Indian Passport is processed and issued by the Indian authorities, Bharat Suvidha ensures its secure and confidential delivery directly to your doorstep, anywhere in the world. You will receive tracking information to monitor your document's journey.
