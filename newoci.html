What is a New OCI Card and who is eligible to apply?

A New OCI (Overseas Citizen of India) Card is a lifelong visa and a form of permanent residency for persons of Indian origin. It is generally eligible for foreign citizens (except Pakistani and Bangladeshi nationals) who were citizens of India on or after 26th January 1950, or who were eligible to become a citizen of India on or after 26th January 1950, or who belonged to a territory that became part of India after 15th August 1947, and their children and grandchildren.

How does Bharat Suvidha simplify the application process for a New OCI Card?

Bharat Suvidha offers a comprehensive online service for New OCI Card applications. We assist with document preparation, form filling, online submission, and follow-up with the authorities. Our goal is to make the process hassle-free, eliminating the need for you to visit any office or embassy.

What documents are typically required for a New OCI Card application?

Commonly required documents include proof of Indian origin (e.g., old Indian passport, birth certificate), current foreign passport, marriage certificate (if applicable), and photographs. The exact list can vary based on individual circumstances, and our experts will provide a tailored checklist.

How long does it take to process a New OCI Card application?

The processing time for a New OCI Card can vary depending on various factors, including the completeness of the application, the workload at the High Commission/Consulate, and any specific verification requirements. While we strive for the fastest possible processing, it typically takes several weeks to a few months. We will keep you updated on the status.

Will my New OCI Card be delivered to my home?

Yes, once your New OCI Card is processed and issued by the Indian authorities, Bharat Suvidha ensures its secure and confidential delivery directly to your doorstep, anywhere in the world. You will receive tracking information to monitor your document's journey.
